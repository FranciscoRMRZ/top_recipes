Cheese omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Olive oil
  • 10g cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl, season with a pinch of salt and black pepper. Then, beat well with a fork or whisk until fully combined.
  2. Place a small pan on a low heat to warm up.
  3. Grate the cheese onto a plate, and set apart.
  4. Add 1/2 tbsp of oil to the pan, then carefully pour in the egg mix.
  5. Tilt the pan to spread the mix evenly, using a fork to swirl it around the pan a little.
  6. When the egg mix begins to cook and firm up, but is a little raw on the top, sprinkle over the cheese.
  7. Using a spatula, ease around the edges of the omelette, then fold it over in half.
  8. When it starts to turn golden brown underneath, remove the pan from the heat and slide the omelette onto a plate.

Recipe taken from Jamie Oliver.
