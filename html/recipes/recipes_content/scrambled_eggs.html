Scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 tbsp cream milk
  • About 2 tbsp of butter
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Lightly whisk the eggs, the milk and a pinch of salt until the mixture is integrated.
  2. Heat a small pan for about two minutes, add the butter and let it melt. Don't allow the butter to brown, or it will discoulour the eggs.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit (without stirring) for 20 seconds. Stir with a wooden spoon, lifting and folding it over the bottom of the pan.
  4. Let it sit for another 10 seconds, then stir and fold again.
  5. Repeat until the eggs are softly set. Remove from the heat and leave for a moment to finish cooking.
  6. Give a final stir and serve the scramble without delay.
