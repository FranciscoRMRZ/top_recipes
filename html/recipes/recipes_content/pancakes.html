Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tspn baking powder
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1/4 tspn salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 3 tbspn butter, melted
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, sugar and salt together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add the milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the pan, using 1/4 cup of mix for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flim and cook until browned on the other side.
  3. Repeat these stemps with the remaining batter.
